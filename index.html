Key Features

Memory Sharing

Create and share your personal gaming stories. Whether it's your first boss victory, an epic clutch moment, or a nostalgic playthrough - every memory deserves to be preserved and celebrated by the community.

Personal Game Library

Build and organize your gaming collection with custom statuses. Track games on your wishlist, mark what you're currently playing, celebrate completed titles, and keep a record of your entire gaming journey.

Community Engagement

Connect with fellow gamers through likes, comments, and follows. Share your reviews, discover popular memories, and build your gaming network. Earn XP and level up as you contribute to the community.

About This Project

My Story

As a passionate gamer since childhood, I've always treasured those unforgettable gaming moments - the thrill of defeating a challenging boss, the joy of discovering a hidden area, or the emotion of completing a story that stayed with me for years.

These memories are often shared on generic social platforms where they get lost in the noise. I wanted to create a dedicated space where gaming memories aren't just posts, but preserved experiences that bring gamers together.

MyMemoryCard was born from this vision: a platform where every gaming memory has a home, where communities form around shared experiences, and where our gaming journeys are celebrated and remembered.
